Ribeaupierre Highschool

It’s at Ribeaupierre Highschool from Ribeauvillé that I obtained my highschool diploma, a beautiful scool that took residence in the ruins of the Ribeaupierre family’s castle on the summit of the city.

Here, I have studied in what used to be the «Social Economics» path which gave particular importance to various knowledge about the way the economy works or how a society is organised.

Business Studies

After highschool, like many young adults, I didn’t have a particular professionnal field which I wanted to aim for. So, I decided I’d work towards a Business Degree in order to give myself skills which would be useful no matter where I end up.

Therefore I did, at Colmar’s IUT, a two-year degree in Commercialisation Techniques in order to acquire general skills in various fields of business. I have also completed the third year of a licence at the FMA to specialise in marketing.

BUT Network and Telecommunications

After those 3 years in business, here I am in a field that is all about electronics and computers. To be completely honest, I have always been really interested by computers, whether it be what we can do with them or how they internally work.

Thanks to various acquaintances working in the field, I could take notice of the professional potential of this field of work, something I hadn’t really considered before. Which isn’t necessarily bad, since I can now evolve in a subject that I’m really passionate about while having a few very useful skills.

Create a company

Whether it be its legal form, the services’ organisation, writing contracts or even managing a team day-to-day, I can build up adn operate any part of a company.

Launch a product

That it is the product’s concept, it’s public image’s creation, the advertisements to present it to the clients or it’s distribution to them, I can put each of those elements in synergy in order to bring out the best chances for it to be a success.

Install / Administer a network

I can physically install as well manage all harware and software making up a LAN, from a PC to a routeur, whithout forgetting the cabling.

Internship THE STORE

My studies in the business field concluded with an internship at THE STORE in Colmar, which is a «concept-store» offering clothes and accessories as well as decorations, and which does it in a way the breaks conventions for a clothes shop.


My task was to advice the manager and to give her suggestions concerning the potential evolutions possible for her shop from now on. To do that, I studied her shop’s environnement and did a survey with the help of her clients.

ALINOVA Competition

My third licence year happened in parallel to the ALNOVA project. It consisted in the creation of a new food product in collaboration with agriculture department as well as the planification et organisation of its launch. While the project didn’t finish in the products being actually launched, the idea was to do it «as if» in order for it to be an actual possibility after the degree.


The ALINOVA project finished with us participating in the TRIAGE (a food innovation competition for the Grand Est) where my group’s product, Bis’Pom, won the public’s and the judges’ votes.

Internship O2

My first professional intership was done in the O2 agency of Colmar, which is a company that is specialised in taking care of homes and accompanying physically restrained people in their day-to-day life.


I had the opportunity to establish and maintain business relationships with various local companies. I have also been able to communicate directly with the customers, as I was in charge of maintaining their loyalty as welle as managing their relationship with our employees.
